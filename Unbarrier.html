Un-barrier

Real time Sign Language translation in Video Calls

Project Overview

A web-based video calling app that uses SimplePeer (WebRTC), TensorFlow.js, and ml5.js to enable conversations between people with disabilities and able-bodied people. Sign language translation to text can be performed in real-time during video calls by users who have trained the system. In assistant mode, the user can have conversational interactions with voice assistants like Alexa
