View Demo

Project Overview

This Women's Safety Android App was built to address the safety concerns for women. The app provides a simple and handy solution to assist females incase she finds herslef stuck in an unfavourable situation . Key features of the app include:

1. Alert System: The user can click the yellow "ALERT ONLY" button when they feel unsafe in a specific location. This action sends the location image to the registered emergency contact.
2. High-Risk Alert: In case of extreme danger, the user can press the red "HIGH RISK" button, which immediately calls the nearby police officer and sends an SMS to the emergency contacts with the user's location.
3. Contact Management: Users can add emergency contact numbers to be notified during critical situations.
4. Personal Details: The app allows users to add personal information that could be useful in case of an emergency.
5. Safety Tips: Users can access safety tips to enhance their awareness and precautions.
